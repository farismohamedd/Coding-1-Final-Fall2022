My Favorite Player's Legacies

Messi,"The little boy from Roasrio, Argentina"

Messi is known to be as one of the greatest players of all time. Messi was born in Rosario, Argentina, he developed a passion to the sport at an early age. His first club was a local one named Grandoli later on his grandmother died, whom he pays tribute to by looking up and pointing at the sky after scoring a goal (as shown in the photo). He then joined the Rosario club and played fir six years, he scored about 500 goals and was a member of "The Machine of '87". At the age of 10 he was diagnoesed with a growth hormone deficiency which was career threatening. At the age of 16, Messi played for Barcelona. 2021 was Messi's final year at Barcelona, he then joined PSG on 10 August, 2021 and is currently playing in the world cup.
